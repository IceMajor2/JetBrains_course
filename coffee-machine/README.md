# coffee-machine
A programmatic prototype of a coffee machine. You are able to simulate a bunch of operations including **buying**, **withdrawing**, **checking & supplementing the equipment**.

You can buy:
* an *espresso* for **$4**
* a *latte* for **$7**
* a *cappuccino* for **$6**

The equipment includes:
* water
* milk
* coffee beans
* disposable cups
* money
